How Jia helps

Not a chat app.
A place to come home to.

A private space designed for warmth — where watching counts as participating, and a small hello is always enough.

01
Postcards

Share a photo, voice note, or short video. No caption required. No pressure to be interesting. Just a small window into your day.

Low effort · High warmth
02
Weekly Recaps

Every week, Jia gathers your family's moments into one view. Check in once, never miss a thing. The weekly heartbeat of your family.

Weekly ritual
03
Overlap Finder

Tell Jia when you're free. It finds the windows when everyone's awake and available — so those calls actually happen.

Time zone aware
04
Watch-only Mode

Parents and grandparents can browse and react without creating anything. Feeling included doesn't require content creation.

Designed for parents
05
Family Nights

Schedule dinner or movie night across time zones. Share your plate. Rate the film. Small rituals that build closeness over time.

Shared experiences
06
Gentle Prompts

"Spot something red this week." Fun, concrete prompts that give everyone something easy to share — never homework, never guilt.
