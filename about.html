Jia means home.

We're a small team of third-culture kids who know what it feels like to love your family deeply, and still feel the distance growing.

We started Jia because we noticed that the tools that exist for staying connected with family weren't designed for families like ours. They were designed for long-distance friends, partners, or the public. None of them understood time zones, cultural differences, or the quiet guilt of not calling enough.

家 (jiā) is the Mandarin word for home and family. It's one of the first characters children learn to write. For us, it carries the weight of everything we're trying to build - a feeling of belonging that distance can't dissolve.

Jia is our attempt to build something that actually works for families across distance. Not another chat app. Not another social feed. A warm, private space that makes staying close feel effortless.

Every feature we design, we test on our own families first. If it doesn't make us feel closer to our parents and siblings, it doesn't ship.

— The Jia team
